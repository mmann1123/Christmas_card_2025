# Save Lives This Holiday Season

A website encouraging friends and family to donate 1% of their income to high-impact international development efforts through [GiveWell.org](https://www.givewell.org/).

## Scan to Visit

![QR Code to Website](qr.png)

## About

This site highlights the humanitarian crisis caused by the discontinuation of USAID funding and shows how individual donations can make a real difference:

- **$2,500** can save one life through malaria prevention
- **$3** provides a malaria net protecting a family for 3+ years
- **1%** of your income can save multiple lives over a decade
